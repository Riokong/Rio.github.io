**Project Overview**  
Built a **NumPy** MLP (even vs odd) and evaluated optimizers.

**Results**  
- GD: **81.25%**, SGD+Momentum: **87.13%**, Adam: **96.82%**

**Tech**: NumPy, ReLU/Sigmoid, BCE loss
